Welcome to SupplyHope

At SupplyHope, we believe that every child deserves access to the tools they need to succeed in school. Our mission is to provide essential school supplies to students in underserved communities, ensuring that they have the resources necessary for a successful education.

Our Cause

SupplyHope is dedicated to removing barriers to education by providing students in underserved communities with the essential school supplies they need to learn, grow, and succeed. We believe that no child should be held back due to a lack of materials.

We work with local schools, community organizations, and volunteers to identify students in need and provide them with backpacks filled with essential supplies. Our goal is to ensure that every child has the tools they need to succeed in school.

We believe that education is a right, not a privilege. By providing students with the supplies they need, we are helping to level the playing field and give every child the opportunity to succeed.

What Supplies Are Needed?

We are currently accepting donations of the following school supplies:

  • Backpacks (new or gently used)
  • Notebooks, folders, binders
  • Pens, pencils, markers, highlighters
  • Calculators, rulers, glue sticks
  • Crayons, colored pencils, erasers
  • Hand sanitizer, tissues, masks

We accept donations of new or gently used supplies as well as monetary contributions. Every item or dollar counts and helps us make a difference.

Thank you for your generosity and support in empowering students and fostering their education.
