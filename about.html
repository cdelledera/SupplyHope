About SupplyHope

SupplyHope is a nonprofit organization committed to bridging the gap in educational equity by supplying students in underserved communities with the essential tools they need to thrive in school.

Our Story

Founded by a group of passionate educators and community leaders, SupplyHope began with a simple goal: ensure that no student is left behind due to a lack of basic school supplies. Since our start, we’ve partnered with local schools, community centers, and generous donors to distribute thousands of backpacks filled with learning essentials.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To empower students by providing access to essential school supplies that foster confidence, creativity, and academic success.

Vision: A future where every child starts school with the tools they need to succeed—no matter their background.

Our Impact

  • Over 5,000 backpacks distributed annually
  • Partnerships with 20+ schools and shelters
  • 100+ active volunteers each year
  • Ongoing mentorship and academic support programs

Meet the Team

Our dedicated team is made up of educators, volunteers, and community advocates who believe in the power of education. Each team member brings their passion and expertise to help SupplyHope grow and serve more students every year.
